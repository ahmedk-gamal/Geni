03 / The Human Story

Born in a
Seminar Room
at AUC.

It started with a seminar on diabetes management at the American University in Cairo. Patients — engineers, teachers, mothers — were sharing one common fear: not the disease itself, but the cost of managing it. Monitoring strips, insulin, specialist visits, insurance rejections.

That room held a truth the insurance industry ignores: the people most motivated to stay healthy are the ones who can least afford to.

Geni-Shield is our answer — built on the principle of زكاة العلم (Zakat Al-Ilm). Knowledge that doesn't give back is incomplete. Every line of code, every smart contract, every AI model we write is our way of giving back to the community that raised us.
