**Geni-Shield** is an AI-driven, decentralized health insurance ecosystem designed to close the protection gap in Egypt. Inspired by a deep human connection at the **AUC Diabetes Seminar**, we transform health behavior into a digital currency.

---

## 🚀 The Vision: "Zakat Al-Ilm" & Community Impact
Born from a mission to **Give Back to the Egyptian Community**, Geni-Shield moves insurance from a static financial burden to a dynamic, rewarding lifestyle. We leverage advanced data science to empower the **11M+ diabetic patients** and **30M+ Gen-Z/Freelancers** who are currently underserved.

## 🧠 Core Technology Stack
- **Privacy-First AI:** Utilizing **Federated Learning** (TensorFlow Federated) to analyze DNA and biomarkers locally on devices.
- **Blockchain Trust:** **Smart Contracts** on Polygon for automated "Geni-Coin" rewards and instant claim payouts.
- **Behavioral Mining:** Real-time sensor fusion to verify physical activity and prevent fraud.
- **Architecture:** Built with **FastAPI**, **PostgreSQL**, and **Flutter** for seamless wearable integration.

## 📊 Market Insight
- **Target:** Egypt & North Africa.
- **Problem:** 70% of youth are uninsured; Insurance companies face high Loss Ratios due to reactive care.
- **Solution:** Reducing insurer risks by **25%** through predictive health modeling and preventative incentives.
